Financial-Approved Slack Users who can receive dollar amounts / margins / rates

Slack users on this list can ask Nora about financial details and get real answers. Everyone else gets a redirect ("I can't share financial details, reach out to John or Mallory"). The output scrubber catches any leaks the model misses.

Proactive Slack Channels where Nora can chime in unsolicited

Strict opt-in per channel. In channels on this list, every non-mention message runs through a stricter Claude gate (defaults to no) before Nora considers chiming in. After any successful proactive post, the channel cools down for 30 minutes.

Joined Slack Threads conversations Nora is currently following

Once Nora replies in a thread, follow-up messages reach her without re-mention until the thread goes stale (5 unaddressed messages or 30 min idle). Untrack any thread she shouldn't be following.
